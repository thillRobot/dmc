		\begin{fleqn}
		Equation of Motion for Mass 1: \\		
		\[ m_1\ddot{x}_1+c_1(\dot{x}_1-\dot{x}_2)+k_1(x_1-x_2)=0 \] 	
		Equation of Motion for Mass 2: \\	
		\[ m_2\ddot{x}_2+k_2x_2-c_1(\dot{x}_1-\dot{x}_2)-k_1(x_1-x_2)=0 \]\vspc
		\end{fleqn}

		It is common to write the equations of motion as a matrix equation. If you are unsure if you have the correct form just multiply it out and should match.\vspace{30mm}
